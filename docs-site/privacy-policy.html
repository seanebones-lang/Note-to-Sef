Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-03-23

Note to Self is a local-first note-taking application published by NextEleven Studios.

Summary

  • Your notes are stored locally on your device.
  • We do not run a backend service for user accounts.
  • We do not sell personal data.
  • We do not include third-party advertising SDKs.

Data We Process

The app can process notes, tags, categories, optional voice input, and optional exported files on your device. This data is not sent to NextEleven Studios by default.

Permissions

The app may request microphone, speech recognition, and file system access for related features that you initiate. Permissions can be revoked in device settings.

Data Sharing

We do not share your note content with third parties. If you export data to another app or cloud service, that action is controlled by you.

Data Retention and Deletion

  • Your data remains on your device until you delete it.
  • Deleting notes in the app removes them from app storage.
  • Uninstalling may remove local app data depending on platform behavior and backups.

Children's Privacy

The app is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Security

We rely on platform-level controls (device encryption, passcodes, sandboxing). You are responsible for securing your device and backups.

International Users

Because data is stored locally by default, international transfer of your note content by us does not normally occur.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Material updates will be reflected by the effective date above.
