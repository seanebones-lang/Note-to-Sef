Support Policy

Effective date: 2026-03-23

This policy describes how support is provided for Note to Self.

Support Channel

GitHub Issues

What Is Covered

  • Reproducible bugs and crashes.
  • Data loss caused by app defects.
  • Installation and update problems.
  • Accessibility issues and major usability blockers.

What Is Not Guaranteed

  • Immediate response times.
  • Custom feature implementation.
  • Support for modified builds not distributed by maintainers.
  • Recovery of data deleted outside app-controlled storage.

Best Effort Targets

  • Critical crash/data integrity: 2 business days for initial response.
  • General bugs: 5 business days for initial response.

Please Include in Reports

  • Device and OS version.
  • App version/build number.
  • Steps to reproduce.
  • Screenshots/logs (without sensitive personal note content).
